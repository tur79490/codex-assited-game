Single-player logic adventure

Lantern Logic Quest

Guide Mira through 10 vivid locations, from bright hills to haunted castle rooms. Solve each gate with your mouse or trackpad, use hints when you need them, and reach the hidden treasure vault.

How to Play

Read each stage question and click one answer.

Use the Hint button whenever you want a clue.

Clear all 10 stages to unlock the treasure chest at the end.

Each chest click takes exactly 1 coin. Taking more than 10 coins resets the quest.
